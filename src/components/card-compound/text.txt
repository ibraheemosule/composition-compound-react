In this card, a prop with name is passed and rendered among its children

pros
no prop drilling
subcompoenents are properties on the Card
Components are more descriptive on where they are to be used
Can choose the component to render and the one not to render
limited imports where it's being used

Cons
Card subcompoenents can still be used outside the Card scope
Card does not know if it's subcompoenents are what is being rendered
Card still doesn't have parent-child prop relationship with children
